About Us

Celebrating two decades of excellence, Al Hazmi International is synonymous with cutting-edge industrial food equipment and packaging solutions in Saudi Arabia. Our journey is marked by a steadfast commitment to quality, catering to diverse clients from niche restaurants to luxurious five-star hotels.

We are a robust solution provider not just in the food industry, but also in industrial racking and general trading for construction materials. We offer comprehensive, end-to-end solutions including meticulous design, sales, installation, and exceptional aftersales service.

Whether you need a single oven or a completely tailored suite, our highly trained engineers stand ready to exceed your expectations with unparalleled dedication.
